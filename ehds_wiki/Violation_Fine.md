# Violation_Fine

Member States must establish effective, proportionate and dissuasive penalties for EHDS infringements.

## Index Citations
- Art. 66(1): "Member States shall lay down the rules on penalties applicable to infringements"
- Art. 66(2): Penalties must be "effective, proportionate and dissuasive"
- Art. 66(4): Penalty types include administrative fines, temporary/permanent bans, cease and desist orders, corrective measures
