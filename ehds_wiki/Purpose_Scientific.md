# Purpose_Scientific

Scientific research is a permitted secondary use purpose under Annex II, subject to HDAB authorisation and ethics committee approval.

## Index Citations
- Art. 54(1): Electronic health data may be processed for "scientific research [...] where such research falls within one of the areas listed in Annex II"
- Art. 54(3): Applicant must demonstrate (a) ethics committee favourable opinion, (b) appropriate research methods, (c) data adequacy and minimisation
