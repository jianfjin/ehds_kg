# HDAB_Approval

Health Data Access Bodies (HDABs) are national authorities that approve or refuse secondary use applications.

## Index Citations
- Art. 59(1): HDABs are responsible for "receiving and processing data applications", "granting or refusing authorisations", "ensuring compliance", and "monitoring the use"
- Art. 59(2): HDABs must be "functionally independent from data holders and data users"
- Art. 59(3): Refusal grounds include non-Annex-II purpose, failure to meet Art. 54(3) requirements, or legal violation
