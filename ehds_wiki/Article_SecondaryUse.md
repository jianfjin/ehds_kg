# Article_SecondaryUse

Directs that health data can be processed for secondary purposes (scientific research, public health, etc.) provided it's approved by the HDAB.

## Index Citations
- Art. 54(2): "Electronic health data may be processed for the purpose of scientific research [...] subject to prior authorisation by the health data access body"
- Art. 33: Principles of proportionality, transparency, scientific integrity, non-discrimination, data minimisation
