# International_Transfer

Cross-border transfer of health data for secondary use must comply with Chapter V safeguards and HDAB oversight.

## Index Citations
- Art. 54(2): "subject to prior authorisation by the health data access body [...] except where the data are made available in an anonymous format"
- Art. 59(1)(c): HDAB must ensure "compliance with the conditions and safeguards laid down in this Chapter"
