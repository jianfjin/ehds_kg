# Article 41 — Transparency and information to data subjects

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A41`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 41  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A41-P1]]

1. Data subjects shall be informed of the intended secondary use of their electronic health data in a concise, transparent, intelligible and easily accessible form.
2. Information shall be provided through the European Health Data Space electronic system.
