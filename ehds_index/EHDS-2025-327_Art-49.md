# Article 49 — Remedies and liability

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A49`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 49  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A49-P1]]

1. Any person who has suffered material or non-material damage as a result of an infringement of this Regulation shall have the right to receive compensation.
2. Data controllers shall be exempt from liability if they prove that they are not responsible for the event giving rise to the damage.
