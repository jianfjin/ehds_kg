# Article 46 — Cooperation with supervisory authorities

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A46`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 46  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A46-P1]]

1. Data controllers shall cooperate, on request, with the supervisory authority in the performance of its tasks.
2. Member States shall ensure that their supervisory authorities have the necessary powers and resources.
