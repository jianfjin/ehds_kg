# Article 42 — Right to object

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A42`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 42  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A42-P1]]

1. Data subjects shall have the right to object to the processing of their electronic health data for secondary use purposes.
2. The right to object shall be exercised free of charge and through the European Health Data Space electronic system.
