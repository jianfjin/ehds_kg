# Article 39 — Conditions for processing for public health purposes

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A39`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 39  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A39-P1]]

1. Processing for public health purposes shall be limited to what is necessary for the protection against serious cross-border threats to health.
2. The competent public health authority shall be responsible for ensuring compliance with this Article.
