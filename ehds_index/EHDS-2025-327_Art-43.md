# Article 43 — Data protection impact assessment

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A43`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 43  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A43-P1]]

1. Prior to the commencement of processing for secondary use, the data controller shall carry out a data protection impact assessment where the processing is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons.
2. The assessment shall be submitted to the competent supervisory authority for consultation where appropriate.
