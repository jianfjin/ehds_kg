# Article 40 — Data quality and integrity

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A40`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 40  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A40-P1]]

1. Data controllers shall ensure that electronic health data processed for secondary use are accurate, complete and up to date.
2. Where data quality issues are identified, the data controller shall take appropriate measures to rectify or delete the data.
