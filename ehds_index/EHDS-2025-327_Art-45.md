# Article 45 — Record of processing activities

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A45`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 45  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A45-P1]]

1. Data controllers shall maintain a record of all processing activities carried out for secondary use purposes.
2. The record shall be made available to the supervisory authority upon request.
