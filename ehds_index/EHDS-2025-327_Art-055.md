# Art. 55 — Permitted purposes for secondary use: public health

## Para 1
Electronic health data may be processed for public health purposes, including:
  (a) disease surveillance, monitoring and early warning;
  (b) assessment of health technologies and health policies;
  (c) development and evaluation of public health interventions.

## Para 2
The processing of electronic health data for public health purposes shall be subject to prior authorisation by the health data access body, unless Union or national law provides for a specific legal basis for such processing without the need for such authorisation.

## Para 3
Public health authorities shall be exempt from the authorisation requirement under paragraph 2 where they process electronic health data within their mandate and in compliance with applicable public health legislation.
