# Article 44 — Security of processing

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A44`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 44  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A44-P1]]

1. Data controllers and processors shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.
2. Measures shall include pseudonymisation, encryption, and ensuring ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability and resilience of processing systems.
