# Article 61 — Modification, suspension and revocation of the data permit

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A61`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 61  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A61-P1]]

1. The Health Data Access Body may modify, suspend or revoke the data permit where:
   (a) the conditions for issuing the permit are no longer met;
   (b) the data controller has infringed the conditions of the permit.
2. The data controller shall be given the opportunity to be heard before a decision to revoke is taken.
