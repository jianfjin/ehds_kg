# Article 47 — Competent supervisory authority

## Index Summary
> **Stable ID:** `EHDS-2025-327-A47`  
> **Regulation:** (EU) 2025/327  
> **Article:** 47  
> **Category:** Chapter V — Secondary Use

## Para 1
[[A47-P1]]

1. Each Member State shall designate one or more supervisory authorities responsible for monitoring the application of this Chapter.
2. The supervisory authority shall act with complete independence in performing its duties.
