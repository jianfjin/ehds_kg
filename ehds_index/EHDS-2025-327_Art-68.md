# Art. 68 — Data access application

## Para 1
The health data access application referred to in Article 67 shall include at minimum:

  (a) a detailed description of the requested electronic health data, including their categories, format, time period and, where applicable, the data holders;

  (b) a description of the intended use of the electronic health data, including whether data linkage is requested across multiple datasets or data holders;

  (c) a description of the safeguards planned to protect the electronic health data, including measures to prevent re-identification;

  (d) the lawful purpose of the secondary use, referencing the applicable provision of Articles 53, 54, or 55;

  (e) the expected duration of the processing;

  (f) the identity and qualifications of the applicant and any person who will have access to the data.

## Para 2
The health data access body shall assess whether the application is complete and whether the conditions for issuing a data permit under Article 69 are met.

## Para 3
Where the application concerns data from multiple data holders, or involves data linkage across datasets, the health data access body shall coordinate the assessment with the relevant data holders and, where applicable, with other health data access bodies.
